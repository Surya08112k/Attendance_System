How It Works

1. Get Your QR Code

Each employee gets a unique QR code linked to their Employee ID from the Employees page.

2. Scan to Attend

Visit the Scanner page and point your camera at the QR code. Attendance is marked instantly.

3. Auto-saved to Excel

All records are instantly saved to an Excel file. Download the report anytime from the dashboard.
